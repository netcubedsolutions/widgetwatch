Starlink WiFi

United Airlines is equipping its fleet with SpaceX Starlink satellite internet, offering gate-to-gate high-speed WiFi. As of February 2026, 258 aircraft across mainline and United Express are Starlink-equipped. Track which planes have Starlink using the interactive Starlink Tracker on the main dashboard.

Interactive Fleet Tools
